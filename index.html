F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When are club meetings?

    Club meetings are weekly on Tuesday at lunchtime. Each week will alternate for either learning the curriculum or working on your project. More details like exact dates, will be sent out later by email.

  • How long are club meetings?

    Club meetings are usually the whole lunch period, or about 30 mins.

  • When will the content of the meeting be posted?

    Expect any form of content to be posted right after the meeting or at the end of the day, by email and it will be published on the website. Questions about how to access it, will be answered in the emails.

  • What is the curriculum?

    Please head to the header, and click More!, next click on Syllabus.

  • Do I need to bring or pay anything to attend this meeting?

    You may need to pay a fee for purchasing an arduino kit. This kit will essentially help you build the project, and will help you participate in the competition. You will also be required to bring your personal computers so you can use the correct Arduino software.

  • Who do I contact if I have any more questions?

    Please contact calhigharduinoclub@gmail.com if you have any further questions.
